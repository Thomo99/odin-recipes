Caramel Slice

Description

This is my famous Caramel Slice recipe. Enjoy.

Ingredients

Base
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup desicated coconut
  • 125g unsalted butter
Caramel Filling
  • 125g unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 395g tin condensed milk
Chocolate Top
  • 200g milk melting chocolate
  • 1tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

Base
  1. Preheat oven to fan 160
  2. Grease pan
  3. Mix together base ingredients
  4. Bake for 12 minutes. Cool in the fridge if you have time.
Caramel
  1. Lower oven to 140
  2. Place butter, sugar and vanilla in a saucepan on medium heat.
  3. Constantly stir/whisk for this.
  4. When bubbles appear, add condensed milk
  5. Stir until bubbling. Once you have big bubbles for about 1 minute, pour over the base.
  6. Bake for 12 minutes.
  7. Let it cool for 20 minutes before putting it in the fridge.
Chocolate
  1. Before the slice goes completely cool, put the chocolate ingredients in a bowl and microwave for 30 second intervals until silky.
  2. Pour over caramel and throw back into the fridge.
  3. The oil means you can cut this whenever without the chocolate cracking.
