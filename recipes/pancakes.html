Pancakes

Description

You know what these are!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Self-Raising Flour
  • 3/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Directions

  1. All ingredients in a bowl
  2. Stir until only just combine. Too much stirring and the cakes won't rise.
  3. butter in a pan on medium heat, followed by mixture
  4. when the top is all bubbled and popped, flip
  5. Give it another 2 minutes and it's done
  6. Rinse, and repeat.
