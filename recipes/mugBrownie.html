Brownie in a Mug

Description

Honestly, we're at a point where I'm over typing. This is pretty self-explanatory.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 1 1/4 tbsp cocoa powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil, coconut oil or melted butter
  • 4 tbsp milk
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • Ice cream or whatever your topping of choice is.

Directions

  1. Place is all in the mug, stir and throw in the mirowave for 90 seconds
  2. Place whatever toppings you want on.
  3. Careful! She's hot!
