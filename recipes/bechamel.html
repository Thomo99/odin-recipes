Bechamel Sauce

Description

This recipe is written as 'per cup' of whole millk used.
Multiply as required.

Also, I use the spices and cheese to taste. You do you, Boo.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 20g butter
  • 12g Flour
  • Cheese as required
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pinch nutmeg
  • Mustard powder

Directions

  1. Butter in a pot on a medium heat. Stir for a minute to let the water evaporate.
  2. Add the flour and stir for a couple minutes. You need to cook the flour, otherwise the sauce will taste like this was the main ingredient.
  3. Add milk in portions. Continuously stir. Add more milk as it thickens.
  4. Season to taste.
